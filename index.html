About Me

Hi, I’m Oliver de Armas, an aspiring aerospace engineer with a strong background in robotics, electronics, and programming. I enjoy tackling complex engineering challenges, whether it’s designing and building robots, developing control systems, or exploring propulsion technologies.

As the electrical lead for my FRC team and a long-time advocate for STEM education, I've built robots, mentored students, and cultivated a deep understanding of teamwork and innovation. My love for space exploration and engineering has driven me to design model rockets, study propulsion systems, and contribute to impactful research in the field of astronomy.

When I'm not designing or building, you can find me mentoring students through STEMquest, stargazing at local astronomy events, or tinkering with my 3D printer to bring creative ideas to life. I believe that through collaboration, curiosity, and determination, we can turn today's dreams into tomorrow's achievements.
